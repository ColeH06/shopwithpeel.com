1. Overview
Peel is a browser extension designed to help users identify lower prices or similar products on other platforms. It provides a comparative experience between a product you're currently viewing and listings found on third-party platforms.

2. Platform Scope and Limitations
Peel is currently in beta. At this stage, Peel compares product listings exclusively on eBay. It does not search or compare prices across other retailers at this time, though integrations with additional retailers are planned for the future.

3. Accuracy and Reliability
Due to limitations in product data, naming conventions, and listing inconsistencies, Peel cannot guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or relevance of its comparisons. Results may include but are not limited to:
- Similar but not identical items
- Mismatches in pricing
- Mismatches in product variants (e.g., size, color, model)
- Listings that may not include key components or accessories

We strongly encourage users to review eBay listings carefully before making any purchasing decisions. Peel is a tool meant to assist in product discovery, not a definitive shopping recommendation engine.
