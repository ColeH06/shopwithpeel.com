1. Overview Peel is a browser extension designed to help users identify lower-priced or similar products on other platforms. It provides a comparative experience between a product you're currently viewing and listings found on third-party platforms. 2. Platform Scope and Limitations Peel is currently in beta. At this stage, Peel compares product listings exclusively on eBay.
