About Peel

Learn more about who we are and what we do

Peel is an AI-powered browser extension that helps users save time and money by automatically finding better deals when you shop online.

Our mission is to simplify the online shopping experience and make it easier than ever to find the best deals. By comparing listings across major platforms like Amazon, Walmart, Target, eBay and Best Buy, Peel brings the best options directly to you.

We're a small team passionate and driven to helping people shop smarter and see the full picture, not just what one store wants you to see. We believe savings should be easy, not overwhelming.

If you have any feedback, ideas, or want to join our mission, feel free to reach out: contact.shopwithpeel@gmail.com.
