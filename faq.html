Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Peel

What is Peel?
Peel is a Chrome extension that compares prices across stores and retailers to show you better deals while you shop online.

Is Peel free?
Yes! Peel is 100% free to install and use.

How does Peel make money?

Peel uses affiliate links where available. If you purchase a product through our extension, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Unlike other current shopping savings extensions, Peel is committed to transparency and putting users first. We will never take credit in cases we can't find a better deal.

Which sites does Peel work on?
Peel currently works best with major retailers such as Amazon, Walmart, eBay, Target, and Best Buy. Many more are planned for the future.

What browsers does Peel work on?

Currently, Peel is available as a Chrome extension. Support for additional browsers like Firefox, Edge, and Safari are coming soon.

Does Peel work outside the US?

Peel is primarily designed for US-based stores and pricing. While it may still appear on international product pages, deal comparisons and availability are most accurate for users in the United States. That said, we hope to expand our extension internationally in the near future.

Does Peel track my data?
No. Peel does not collect any personal data or browsing history. It only sends product info needed to generate deal comparisons.
