Our Mission

Peel is an AI-powered Chrome extension that helps users save time and money by automatically finding better deals when you shop online.

Our mission is to simplify the online shopping experience and make it easier than ever to find the best deals. By comparing listings across popular sites like Amazon, Walmart, Target, eBay, Best Buy and more, Peel brings the best options directly to you.

We're a small team passionate and driven to helping people shop smarter and see the full picture, not just what one store wants you to see. We believe savings should be easy, not overwhelming.
